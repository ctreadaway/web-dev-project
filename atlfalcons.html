Current Falcons record for the 2025 season: 3-8.

Team Overview

The Atlanta Falcons are a professional football team based in Atlanta, Georgia. They compete in the NFC South division of the NFL.

Falcons History Timeline

  1. 1966: The Atlanta Falcons were established as an NFL expansion team.
  2. 1998: The Falcons reached their first Super Bowl appearance, but lost to the Denver Broncos.
  3. 2016: The Falcons made it to Super Bowl LI but were defeated by the New England Patriots in a historic comeback.
  4. 2025: The Falcons are rebuilding with new talent and a focused strategy.
