Team Overview

The Atlanta Hawks are a professional basketball team based in Atlanta, Georgia. They play in the NBA's Eastern Conference, in the southeast division.

Hawks History Timeline

  1. 1968: The Atlanta Hawks were founded as the Atlanta Hawks after moving from St. Louis.
  2. 1980: The Hawks made their first appearance in the NBA playoffs.
  3. 2015: The Hawks finished first in the Eastern Conference with a 60-22 record.
  4. 2025: Hawks have secured a play in spot in the playoffs and have a promising future with a lot of young talent.
